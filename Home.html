Mission Statement

Our mission is to preach and teach the Gospel of Jesus Christ according to the age old tenents of the Holy Catholic Church.  

We all come together to worship as one. For all peoples, in all places, in all ages, we come together as One Church, One Body in Christ to worship. 


Coming together as a community, and as a family, to worship is a visible sign of unity and fraternal love, sharing “One Lord, One Baptism, and One Faith.  



Rebuilding Our Parish
Update for October 13th, 2017


The structural repair of the roof trusses has been completed in accordance with the engineer’s specifications. The exterior shingles, many of which suffered damage during the fire, were removed and the new shingles were delivered Wednesday. Our expectation is that the roof will be completed before the end of the month. The debris trapped within the crevices of the Church ceiling has been removed.

Reconstruyendo Nuestra Parroquia
Actualización para el 13 de Octubre de 2017

La reparación estructural de las armazones de techo se ha completado en acuerdo con las especificaciones del ingeniero. Las tejas exteriores, muchas que sufrieron daños durante el incendio, fueron removidas y las nuevas tejas fueron entregadas el miércoles. Esperamos que el techo será completado antes de fin de mes. Los escombros atrapados dentro de las grietas del techo de la Iglesia han sido removidos.
