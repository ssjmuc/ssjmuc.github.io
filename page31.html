SPECIAL INVITATION FOR JANUARY 14

In June, our Archbishop, Cardinal Joseph Tobin,
approved the reconstruction of our parish church.
Since that time, a committee of parishioners along
with Father Aro Nathan and Father Guillermo
Mora, has gathered numerous times with the
archdiocesan architect, Mr. James Hundt, to
develop the reconstruction plans for the temple.
According the liturgical guidelines, the committee
considered different models presented by Mr.
Hundt.

On Sunday, January 14th, Mr. Hundt will make a
presentation to the entire parish community of the
model which has received the greatest support to
date. The presentation will be made in English and
Spanish.

It is important that every parishioner have this
information readily available, as well as the
opportunity to express an opinion on the various
details personally considered important in the
reconstruction of the temple. The members of the
committee will make note of suggestions and
views expressed by the parish community before
making its final recommendation.
To facilitate the attendance at this presentation the
Mass Schedule will be different on Sunday,
January 14th.

9:30AM: Mass (English)
10:30AM: Presentation of the Proposed Plan
for the Church Renovation
12:00PM: Mass (Spanish)

Please note there will only be one mass in Spanish.
We all love our parish community, and are thankful
that our church can be rebuilt after the
devastating fire that it suffered on March 4, 2017.
We are all encouraged to attend this presentation.
It is necessary to remain informed and to express
what we each find to be important (or preferred) in
the restoration, in order to make our parish church
the best possible home for all the liturgical
celebrations of our community of Ss. Joseph and
Michael.
