Mission Statement

Our mission is to preach and teach the Gospel of Jesus Christ according to the age old tenents of the Holy Catholic Church.  

We all come together to worship as one. For all peoples, in all places, in all ages, we come together as One Church, One Body in Christ to worship. 


Coming together as a community, and as a family, to worship is a visible sign of unity and fraternal love, sharing “One Lord, One Baptism, and One Faith.  


Rebuilding Our Parish
Update for September 2017


We are roughly 75% of the way toward rebuilding the trusses along the roofline. Five of the seven sections have been replaced and work at the transept area has begun so we are on target for completion by the beginning of October.

Reconstruyendo Nuestra Parroquia
Actualización Septiembre 2017

Estamos aproximadamente el 75% del camino hacia la reconstrucción de las armaduras a lo largo de la línea del techo. Cinco de las siete secciones han sido reemplazadas y el trabajo en el área del transepto ha comenzado, así que estamos a tiempo para terminar a principios de octubre.
