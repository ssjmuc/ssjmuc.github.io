Parish Announcements 
Ss. Joseph & Michael Church
December 9-10, 2017

UNIVERSAL CHURCH DIRECTORIES

Universal Church Directories will come to our Sacred Heart Chapel on Monday December 18th and Tuesday December 19th beginning at 2:00pm to professionally photograph our families and give us a free 8x10 portrait and directory. There are plenty of spaces available. Families of all sizes are welcome to get photographed for a free 8x10 portrait. For more information, please refer to this week’s bulletin.


CHRISTMAS MASS 
SCHEDULE

Parishioners that receive the offertory envelopes at home have begun to receive the Christmas Mass Schedule. Unfortunately, the schedules are sent to print months in advance and some of the details have now changed. Please follow the Christmas Schedule provided in this week’s bulletin, flyers, and on our website.


CHRISTMAS 
PLAY

We invite you to come rediscover the true meaning of Christmas. The children of our parish will perform our annual Christmas play on Sunday, December 10th at 3:00pm at the Mother Seton School Gymnasium. All are invited.




CANDIDATE INTERVIEWS

We are currently interviewing candidates to join our Parish Council Committee as well as our Parish Financial Council Committee. The Parish Council meets a few times a year to review and discuss programs and issues related to parish life and the Parish Financial Council meets throughout the year with the pastor to review and manage parish finances. All committee work leads to building a faith community, proclaiming the Word of God, calling people to prayer and motivating people to serve others. If you would like to discuss the possibilities of volunteering your time and talents, please contact Father Nathan.



BULLETIN

Please take home with you this weekend’s bulletin which contains a detailed schedule and the locations for various activities and events in our parish.
