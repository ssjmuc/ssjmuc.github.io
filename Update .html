Update for 
November 6th, 2017

Roof 

The new roof shingles were installed work was completed by the end of October as scheduled. Scaffold will remain in place for the duration of the exterior renovation on the steeple and tower. The debris from the roof damage left between the outer roof and the Church ceiling has been removed.

Architects/Engineers

There was a meeting on October 24th with the Office of PMA, Foresight Architects and Paul Beck (structural engineer). Mr. Beck will oversee the steeple replacement and tower repair and renovation. Foresight Architects presented several alternative floor plans for the parish committee along with a review of potential lighting plan scenarios. It is hoped that the committee will decide on a collaborative approach for both sometime during this month. On Thursday of this week Mr. Hundt met with Father Dente from the Archdiocesan Office of Divine Worship to discuss the options at hand.

 A proposal to install a full-height aluminum replacement steeple was also reviewed by the committee. The style and aesthetics of the steeple will be virtually identical to the original, however, decisions must still be made with respect to composition (copper or aluminum). A final decision should be reached at the next meeting, which is scheduled for November 15th.

Plastering and Painting

Competitive bids should be received by early December and we are still on target to confirm a comprehensive timeline for execution and completion by January 1st.

As mentioned, our estimate for the entire scope of work is six to eight months from start to finish and we will continue to update this timeline as the project evolves. You are welcome to e-mail any questions to either
