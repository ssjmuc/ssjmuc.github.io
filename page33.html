Rebuilding Our Parish
Update for September 2017

 Roof trusses

We are roughly 75% of the way toward rebuilding the trusses along the roofline. Five of the seven sections have been replaced and work at the transept area has begun so we are on target for completion by the beginning of October. 

Architects/Engineers

Foresight Architects have been retained to develop plans and specifications for all aspects of the restoration project including the liturgical design of the worship space. Representatives from Foresight have already met with Father Nathan and the parish advisory committee to get some preliminary insight as to how to go about gaining a consensus of the preferences of the parish pertaining to restoration plans and design. Foresight has also had a meeting last week with Father Dente and Monsignor Groncki from the Office of Divine Worship to gather their direction and feedback. We also expect to retain an HVAC Engineer this week for the purpose of receiving their guidance for the specifications of the new HVAC systems. We should also receive final word on the structural engineerâ€™s inspection of structural integrity of the plaster ceiling.

Steeple & Tower

Foresight Architects are in the process of gathering information and comparative pricing on steeple repair and replacement. We will review this information at the next meeting of the parish advisory committee, which is scheduled to take place next week.

We will continue to provide monthly updates going forward and post them on the parish home page.
