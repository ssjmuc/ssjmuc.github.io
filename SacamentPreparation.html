To live, we must daily break the body and shed the blood of Creation. When we do this knowingly, lovingly, skillfully, reverently, it is a sacrament. When we do it ignorantly, greedily, clumsily, destructively, it is a desecration.

Requirements / Requisitos

First Reconciliation and First Communion:

A child learns by observing the behavior of his or her parents. This includes the behavior and priorities they have including coming to Sunday Mass and experiencing time for prayer and a growing relationship with God.If you wish to have your children continue their initiation into the Catholic Church, we advise to enroll your children in

 Sacrament Preparation.

We expect you to attend Mass regularly, by every family preparing their  children for First Communion. Children  generally receive immediate preparation for  First Reconciliation and First Communion  

Confirmation

It is the expectation of the parish that each young person wishing  to be confirmed has received ongoing religious instructions in  their grades. In addtion, it's expected that the young person attends Sunday Mass each week.
